Manali


Manali is a breathtaking hill station nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh. Known for its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and peaceful atmosphere, it is a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  1. Rohtang Pass – A snowy paradise with stunning views
  2. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports like paragliding
  3. Hadimba Temple – A serene and spiritual wooden temple
  4. Old Manali – Known for its chill cafes and vibe
  5. Beas River – Perfect for relaxing and scenic beauty
  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali dish
  • Trout Fish – A local delicacy
  • Thukpa – A warm and comforting noodle soup
  • Momos – Steamed dumplings loved by everyone
  • Babru – A Himachali-style kachori
